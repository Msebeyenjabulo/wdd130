Overview

Purpose

The primary purpose of creating a website for our company is to increase our visibility and make it easier for potential customers to discover the experiences we offer. Our website will provide detailed information about our various rafting trips, pricing, and package options. Additionally, implementing an online booking system will enhance customer convenience and make our admin processes easier and faster.

Audience

Our target audience consists of adventure enthusiasts aged 18 to 50 who enjoy thrilling experiences and outdoor activities. They are financially stable and seek exciting rafting trips that cater to their preferences and needs. Our website should provide clear information about our trips, pricing, safety, and equipment, while offering convenient online bookings. Most users will likely access the site via mobile devices, so a mobile-friendly design is essential for their convenience.
